AUTO TRANSPORT

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

SIZE & WEIGHT

Vehicles that are heavier, longer, or higher than the typical car require special accommodations. Therefore, shipping these autos will cost extra

VEHICLE CONDITION

Special equipment will be needed to load inoperable autos. In order to be loaded with a winch, your immobile car must be able to roll, steer, and brake. The cost of shipping these cars will be higher.

LOCATION

There will be a lot more Carriers operating in major towns and cities. Costs are consequently lower than in more remote areas. The Carrier might have to divert from its usual trucking lane to include some rural areas on a cargo route. Your total quote includes the additional time and gasoline.

SHIPPING DISTANCE

Overall, shorter distances are more cost-effective, but the cost per mile is higher. Despite costing less per mile, longer distances are generally more expensive.

SUPPLY & DEMAND

Due to potential bidding wars amongst customers who want their vehicle loaded first as a priority shipment, transportation costs start to rise when there are a lot of automobiles waiting for transportation on a route and few drivers available.

SEASONALITY

Winter shipping of a vehicle will cost higher because of the damage that snow and ice do to numerous trucking lanes.

PRIOR TO TRANSPORT

PREPPING YOUR VEHICLE

Car Condition

Keep a record of your car’s state. If there are any leaks or mechanical issues, make sure the driver is aware of them.

Gas & Fluids

You can fill the gas tank up to 1/4 full. Refill all of the vehicle’s fluids.

Battery & Tires

Ensure that your battery is fully charged. To have completely inflated tyres.

Personal Belongings

Your personal belongings can fit inside the trunk of the car up to 100 pounds. Prior to pickup, be sure to get preapproval.

Removable Items

Must remove the Remove the E-ZPass. All detachable components and bespoke products (leave your spare tire & jack).

Security System

Any security systems that are placed in your car should be turned off or given instructions.
